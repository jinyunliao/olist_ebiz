# Pricing & Packages (Example)

Below are example pricing tiers for engagements of different scope. Use these as a starting point and customize by client size and complexity.

## Pricing tiers (illustrative)

- **Starter** — Implementation-only
  - Deliverables: Core models, tests, and runbook
  - Timeline: 4–8 weeks
  - Price: $25k–$75k (fixed)

- **Growth** — Implementation + 3 months support
  - Deliverables: Everything in Starter + monthly ops and one prioritized improvement
  - Timeline: 6–12 weeks
  - Price: $75k–$150k + $5k–$10k/month retainer

- **Enterprise** — Implementation + 12-month retainer
  - Deliverables: Full implementation, monitoring, SLA, prioritized roadmap
  - Timeline: 3–6 months
  - Price: $150k–$500k + $10k–$30k/month retainer

## Notes on reaching $1M+ revenue
- Top earners combine fixed-price implementations + recurring retainers + multiple enterprise clients. Aim for a mix of 5–10 retainers or several multi-project enterprise clients to scale revenue.

## Payment terms
- 30% on kickoff, 40% on delivery of core milestone, 30% on handover and acceptance (negotiable for enterprise contracts)
